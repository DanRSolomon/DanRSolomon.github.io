About Me

Welcome to my website. My name is Dan Solomon. I've had a number of different jobs over the years in a wide variety of fields. Photography, retail, hospitality, and production. My interests are likewise quite varying: aviation, astronomy, photography. This has made finding a career path quite difficult for me. However, I recently started learning web development and I'm loving it.


I was born and raised in Phoenix, AZ where I attended South Mountain High School. Through their Aerospace Department, I was able to graduate high school with my Private Pilot's License. My interest in photography really blossomed while on a trip to New Zealand and Australia. I learned photography by shooting film and had the opportunity to learn how to develop film and print my own black and white images. I have since moved on to digital photogaphy where I mostly shoot landscape and aviation images. I'm a single father and couldn't love my son more, so of course, he's been the focus of most of my photography lately.
